Visit Liyue

Inspired by China's Landscape, it's scenery is sure to amaze you.

Top three activities to do in Liyue

Attend the Lantern Rite Festival

Lantern Rite is a festival in Liyue celebrated along side Chinese New Years, on the first full moon of the year and for five days.

Ride a Bamboo Raft

Ride Bosu's raft at Yilong Wharf or Sun Rao's raft at Qiaoying Village with a friend to experience a beautiful scenery and short song based off of Chinese culture.

Take a Tour of the Chasm

This Chasm once served as a battleground with the fate Liyue and neighboring nations on the line.
